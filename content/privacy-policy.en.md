# Privacy Policy and Cookie Notice

## Data Controller

The data controller is the owner of the commercial establishment whose contact information is available in the "Info" section of the menu.

## Data Collected

This website is a simple menu viewing application and **does not actively collect personal data** from users.

### Technical Data

For the proper functioning of the application, the following preferences may be stored locally in the browser (localStorage):

- **Preferred language** (Italian/English)
- **Dietary preferences** (vegetarian, vegan)
- **Allergens to exclude**

This information is stored exclusively on the user's device and is not transmitted to external servers.

## Cookies

This site **does not use cookies** to track users or for advertising purposes.

### Technical Cookies

The application may only use strictly necessary technical cookies to:

- Ensure the proper functioning of the website
- Store user preferences

The menu may be loaded from Google Sheets. In this case, the connection to Google is subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

In accordance with GDPR, users have the right to:

- Access their data

- Delete their data

To exercise these rights, contact the data controller using the contact details available in the Info section.

This policy may be updated periodically. We recommend checking it regularly.
